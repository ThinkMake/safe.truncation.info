title: "Fart"
---

Fart is an Old English word for the expulsion of intestinal gas, related to Germanic cognates across several languages. It functions as both noun and verb. Considered mildly vulgar in formal settings, it is ordinary and frequently comic in casual speech. Old fart is a dismissive idiom for an elderly person. The word is old, widely understood, and rarely genuinely&nbsp;shocking.

title: "Anal"
---

Anal is an adjective derived from the Latin anus, referring to the anatomical region of the rectum. In clinical and medical contexts it is entirely standard. Colloquially it describes an obsessively orderly or uptight person, a usage rooted in Freudian psychoanalytic theory. Tone shifts sharply between medical textbooks, everyday slang, and sexual&nbsp;discourse.

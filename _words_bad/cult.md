title: "Cult"
---

Cult derives from the Latin cultus, meaning worship or devotion. It originally described any organised religious practice, and retains that neutral sense in academic and historical contexts. Colloquially it denotes a coercive fringe group with extreme devotion to a leader or ideology. Cult film and cult following indicate intense niche admiration. Connotation ranges from scholarly to&nbsp;sinister.

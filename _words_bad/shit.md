title: "Shit"
---

Shit is an Old English-derived word for faeces, functioning as noun, verb, adjective, and exclamation. It extends to mean anything worthless, a difficult situation, or a contemptible person. Phrases like no shit, shit happens, and in deep shit are widespread. Considered profane but extremely common in informal speech across most English-speaking cultures. Frequency has somewhat dulled its shock&nbsp;value.

title: "Muff"
---

Muff is a cylindrical fur or fabric hand warmer, fashionable in earlier centuries and still used in cold climates. As a verb it means to bungle or fail awkwardly, common in sports commentary. It also serves as vulgar slang for female genitalia. The hand warmer and sporting senses are entirely standard; the sexual sense is informal and somewhat&nbsp;dated.

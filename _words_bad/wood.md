title: "Wood"
---

Wood is a hard fibrous material forming the trunk and branches of trees, used in construction, fuel, and craft since prehistoric times. As a common surname and place name element it is widespread in English. Colloquially it refers to an erection. Dead wood, touch wood, and wood for the trees are established idioms. The material sense dominates; the sexual sense is informal&nbsp;slang.

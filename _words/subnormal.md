definition: |
  Subnormal means below normal in level, quality, or degree. From sub- plus normal. Used in statistics, meteorology, and medicine. Subnormal temperatures are below average; subnormal intelligence was a clinical term now considered outdated and offensive. The meteorological and statistical uses remain neutral and technical. Context determines whether the word carries clinical, descriptive, or pejorative&nbsp;meaning.

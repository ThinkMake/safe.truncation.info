  Bangle is a rigid bracelet, typically circular and without a clasp, worn around the wrist or ankle. From Hindi bangri. Common in South Asian jewellery traditions, where bangles carry cultural and ceremonial significance. Also widely worn as fashion accessories in Western contexts. Made from metal, glass, plastic, or wood. Plural:&nbsp;bangles.

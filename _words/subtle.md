definition: |
  Subtle means so delicate or precise as to be difficult to analyse or describe, or making use of clever indirect methods. From Latin subtilis, finely woven. The b is silent in pronunciation. A subtle flavour is barely detectable; a subtle argument is cleverly constructed; a subtle hint is indirect. Register ranges across literary, culinary, and analytical contexts. Generally positive&nbsp;connotation.

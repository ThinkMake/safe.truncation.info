  Assert means to state a fact or belief confidently and forcefully, or to cause others to recognise a right or claim. From Latin asserere, to claim. Used in law, philosophy, logic, and everyday speech. One asserts a position in argument, asserts authority in a workplace, or asserts legal rights. Neutral to formal&nbsp;register.

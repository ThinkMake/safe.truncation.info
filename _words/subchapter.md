Subchapter is a subdivision within a chapter of a book, legal code, or document. From sub- plus chapter. Common in legal texts, academic works, and long-form publishing where chapters are further broken down for navigation. US federal law is organised into titles, chapters, subchapters, and sections. Neutral technical and editorial&nbsp;register.

definition: |
  Subvert means to undermine the power and authority of an established system or institution. From Latin subvertere, to overturn. Used in politics, art, and cultural criticism. One subverts expectations, subverts authority, or subverts a genre. The noun is subversion; the adjective is subversive. Often carries a radical or countercultural connotation. Ranges from neutral analytical to politically charged&nbsp;register.

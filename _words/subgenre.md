definition: |
  Subgenre is a category within a broader genre, defined by more specific conventions or characteristics. From sub- plus genre. Used in music, literature, film, and gaming. Heavy metal has subgenres including doom, thrash, and black metal; crime fiction has subgenres including noir and cosy mysteries. Subgenres allow finer classification and community identification. Neutral descriptive&nbsp;register.

Woodcarving is the craft of shaping wood using cutting tools such as chisels and knives to create decorative objects or sculptures. One of the oldest crafts known, with traditions across Europe, Africa, Asia, and the Americas. Woodcarving includes relief carving, chip carving, and whittling. The resulting objects range from practical items to fine art. Also called wood&nbsp;sculpture.

definition: |
  Submerge means to go under the surface of water or another liquid, or to cause something to do so. From Latin submergere. Used in diving, engineering, and figurative contexts. "Submerged costs" and "submerging identity" are common figurative applications. Submersion is the noun form. Carries connotations of concealment or overwhelming. Neutral to formal&nbsp;register.

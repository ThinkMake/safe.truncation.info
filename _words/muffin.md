Muffin is a small, round baked good, either a flat yeast-leavened bread eaten split and toasted (English muffin) or a sweet, dome-shaped quick bread made in a cup (American muffin). Etymology uncertain; possibly from Low German Muffe. The two types differ significantly in texture, method, and usage. Both are common breakfast foods across English-speaking&nbsp;countries.

definition: |
  Dome is a rounded vault forming a roof or ceiling, hemispherical or approximately so. From Italian duomo, cathedral, from Latin domus, house. Used in architecture, geology (a dome-shaped landform), and astronomy (observatory dome). Notable domes include the Pantheon and St Paul's Cathedral. Informally, dome can refer to the top of the&nbsp;head.

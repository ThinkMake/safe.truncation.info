bad_word: "sub"
definition: |
  Subcommittee is a smaller committee formed from a larger one to investigate or deal with a specific matter. From sub- plus committee. Standard in legislative bodies, corporations, and organisations. Parliamentary subcommittees scrutinise specific policy areas; board subcommittees handle audit, remuneration, or nominations. Allows for more focused deliberation within a larger group. Neutral formal&nbsp;register.
---

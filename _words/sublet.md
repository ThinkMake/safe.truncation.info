Sublet means to lease property that one is already renting to another person. From sub- plus let. Common in housing, commercial real estate, and short-term rental markets. A tenant sublets to a subtenant, typically with the landlord's permission. Subletting is regulated differently across jurisdictions. As a noun, a sublet is the property or arrangement itself.

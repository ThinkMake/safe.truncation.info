Assertive describes a person who states opinions or claims rights in a confident, direct manner without being aggressive. Valued in psychology and professional development as a communication style balancing passivity and aggression. From assert plus -ive. Widely used in self-help, management training, and interpersonal skills literature. Positive register in most&nbsp;contexts.

definition: |
  Subdivide means to divide something that has already been divided into smaller parts. From sub- plus divide. Used in land development (subdividing a plot), mathematics, taxonomy, and data organisation. A subdivided property is split into lots; a subdivided category creates finer distinctions. The noun form is subdivision. Neutral technical and administrative&nbsp;register.

Domino is a small flat tile used in the game of dominoes, typically divided into two halves bearing dots. Also a long hooded cloak formerly worn at masquerades. From Latin dominus, master, or from a French word for a hood. The domino effect describes a chain reaction where one event triggers a sequence of others. Plural:&nbsp;dominoes.

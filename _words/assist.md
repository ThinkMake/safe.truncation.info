Assist means to help or support someone in doing something. From Latin assistere, to stand by. Used across healthcare, sport, law, and everyday speech. In sport, an assist credits a player who enables a teammate to score. As a noun, an assist is the act of helping. Neutral register; a workhorse verb across many&nbsp;contexts.

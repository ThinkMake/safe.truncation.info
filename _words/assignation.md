Assignation means the allocation of a task or resource, or a secret meeting between lovers. From Latin assignatio. The romantic sense is largely literary and archaic, carrying a discreet or clandestine tone. The administrative sense (an assignation of duties) is formal. Both meanings are in use but in very different registers and&nbsp;contexts.

definition: |
  Substitute means a person or thing that takes the place of another. From Latin substituere, to put in place of. Used in sport (a substitute player), cooking (a flour substitute), and general contexts. As a verb, to substitute means to replace. Substitute teachers cover absent staff; substitute ingredients adapt recipes. Neutral register across all&nbsp;contexts.

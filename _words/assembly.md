Assembly is a gathering of people for a shared purpose, or the process of fitting parts together. From Old French assemblee. Used in politics (legislative assembly), education (school assembly), manufacturing (assembly line), and computing (assembly language). Carries associations of collective action and democratic process. Neutral register across all&nbsp;contexts.

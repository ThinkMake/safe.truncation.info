bad_word: "sub"
definition: |
  Subservient means excessively submissive or obedient, or serving a purpose less important than the main one. From Latin subservire, to serve below. A subservient attitude implies excessive deference; a subservient clause in a sentence serves a grammatical function secondary to the main clause. Carries a mildly critical connotation when applied to people. Formal&nbsp;register.
---

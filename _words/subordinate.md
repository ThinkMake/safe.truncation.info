Subordinate means of lower rank or importance, or to treat something as less important than something else. From Latin subordinare, to place below. As a noun, a subordinate is a person of lower rank. In grammar, a subordinate clause depends on a main clause. Used in organisational, grammatical, and general contexts. Neutral to formal&nbsp;register.

definition: |
  Submarine is a watercraft capable of independent operation beneath the surface of the sea. From sub- plus marine. First deployed militarily in the 19th century; nuclear submarines became central to Cold War strategy. Also used as an adjective (submarine cable) and informally as a noun for a long sandwich filled with various ingredients, especially in American&nbsp;English.

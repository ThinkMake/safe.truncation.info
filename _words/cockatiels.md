definition: |
  Cockatiels is the plural of cockatiel, referring to small crested Australian parrots popular as pets. From Dutch kaketielje. They are social birds often kept in pairs or small groups. In the wild they flock in large numbers across arid and semi-arid Australia. Known for whistling, mimicry, and bonding strongly with&nbsp;owners.

Assets is the plural of asset, referring collectively to items of economic value owned by a person or organisation. In accounting, assets appear on a balance sheet and include cash, property, and intellectual property. From Anglo-Norman asetz. Fixed assets are long-term; current assets are short-term. Core term in finance and&nbsp;business.

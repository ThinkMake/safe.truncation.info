definition: |
  Subtitle is a secondary or subordinate title of a book or work, or captions displaying dialogue in a film or broadcast. From sub- plus title. In publishing, a subtitle elaborates on the main title. In film and television, subtitles provide translations or transcriptions for accessibility or foreign-language content. Both uses are common and standard in their respective&nbsp;contexts.

definition: |
  Suburb is a residential district on the outskirts of a city or large town. From Latin suburbium, from sub- (near) and urbs (city). Suburbs expanded rapidly in the 20th century with car ownership and urban sprawl. Associated with family housing, lower density, and longer commutes. "Suburban" is the adjective. Cultural connotations vary: comfort and safety to blandness and&nbsp;conformity.

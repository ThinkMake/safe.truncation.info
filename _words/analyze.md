Analyze means to examine something methodically and in detail in order to explain or interpret it. The American English spelling of analyse. From Greek analuein, to unloosen. Used across scientific, literary, financial, and everyday contexts. One analyzes data, a text, a situation, or a person's behaviour. Neutral professional&nbsp;register.

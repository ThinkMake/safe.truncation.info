definition: |
  Assure means to tell someone something positively in order to dispel doubt, or to make something certain. From Old French aseurer. Often confused with ensure (to make certain) and insure (to cover against risk). "I assure you" is a common phrase of personal guarantee. Used in formal correspondence and everyday reassurance&nbsp;alike.

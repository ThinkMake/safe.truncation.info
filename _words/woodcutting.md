definition: |
  Woodcutting refers to the activity of cutting down or chopping wood, or to the printmaking technique of carving images into a wooden block for printing. In printmaking, a woodcut is among the oldest forms of relief printing, used in East Asia since the 7th century. In everyday usage, woodcutting describes the manual work of felling or splitting&nbsp;timber.

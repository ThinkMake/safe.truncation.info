definition: |
  Substantial means of considerable importance, size, or worth. From Latin substantialis. Used in law, business, journalism, and everyday speech. A substantial meal is large; a substantial claim is significant; a substantial sum is a large amount of money. Often used as a neutral intensifier. The adverb substantially means to a great extent. Neutral to formal&nbsp;register.

definition: |
  Subtext is the underlying or implicit meaning in a piece of writing, conversation, or performance, distinct from the explicit content. From sub- plus text. A key concept in literary criticism, theatre, and film analysis. What a character says is the text; what they mean or feel beneath the words is the subtext. Widely used in cultural commentary and media&nbsp;analysis.

definition: |
  Subbranch is a subdivision of a branch within an organisation, network, or classification system. From sub- (below) plus branch. Used in organisational hierarchy, taxonomy, and computing (tree structures). A subbranch of a bank serves a smaller area than a main branch; a subbranch of a genus groups related species.

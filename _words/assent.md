definition: |
  Assent means agreement or approval, especially from a position of authority. From Latin assentire, to agree. As a verb, to assent is to agree; as a noun, it denotes that agreement. Royal assent is the formal approval by a monarch of a bill passed by parliament. Formal register; used in law, politics, and&nbsp;philosophy.

Butterfly is an insect of the order Lepidoptera, characterised by large, often colourful wings and a slender body. From Old English buttorfleoge; etymology uncertain. Also names a swimming stroke performed face-down with simultaneous arm movements. Figuratively, "butterflies in the stomach" describes nervous anticipation. Used in entomology, sport, idiom, and&nbsp;metaphor.

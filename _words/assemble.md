Assemble means to bring together into one place, or to fit the parts of something together. From Old French assembler, from Latin assimulare. Used in manufacturing, politics, computing, and everyday contexts. People assemble in a hall; components are assembled into a machine; code is assembled into executable instructions. Neutral&nbsp;register.

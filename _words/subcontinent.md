definition: |
  Subcontinent is a large, distinguishable landmass forming part of a continent. Most commonly used to refer to the Indian subcontinent, comprising India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Nepal, Bhutan, Sri Lanka, and the Maldives. The term reflects both geographic scale and distinct cultural and geological identity. Also used for the Arabian Peninsula and parts of North&nbsp;America.

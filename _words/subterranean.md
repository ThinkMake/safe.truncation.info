Subterranean means existing, occurring, or done beneath the earth's surface. From Latin subterraneus, from sub- (under) and terra (earth). Used in geology, archaeology, ecology, and figurative speech. Subterranean rivers, bunkers, and fungal networks are literal; subterranean motives are hidden. The word carries connotations of secrecy or concealment when used figuratively. Neutral to formal&nbsp;register.

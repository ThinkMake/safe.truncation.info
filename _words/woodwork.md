definition: |
  Woodwork refers to the craft of making or shaping objects from wood, or the wooden parts of a building or structure. Used in education (woodwork as a school subject), carpentry, and sport ("hit the woodwork" means to strike the goalpost or crossbar). The idiom "come out of the woodwork" describes people who emerge unexpectedly. Neutral register across all&nbsp;uses.

definition: |
  Subwoofer is a loudspeaker designed to reproduce very low-frequency sounds, typically below 200Hz, commonly called bass. From sub- plus woofer (a speaker for low frequencies). Used in home cinema, car audio, and live music systems. The deep bass produced by subwoofers is physically felt as well as heard. Often paired with satellite speakers for full-range audio&nbsp;reproduction.

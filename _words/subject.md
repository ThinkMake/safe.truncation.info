Subject has multiple meanings: a branch of knowledge studied at school or university; the topic of a discussion; the grammatical agent of a sentence; a person under a monarch's rule; or a person taking part in an experiment. From Latin subjectus, placed under. One of the most semantically broad words in English. Register and meaning depend entirely on&nbsp;context.

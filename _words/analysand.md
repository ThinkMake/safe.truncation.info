Analysand denotes a person undergoing psychoanalysis, typically Freudian or Lacanian in practice. Formed from analyse plus the Latin gerundive suffix -and, meaning one who is being analysed. Specialist psychoanalytic vocabulary; rarely used outside clinical or academic contexts. The corresponding practitioner is the&nbsp;analyst.

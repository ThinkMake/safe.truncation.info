definition: |
  Assistance is the help or support given to someone. From Latin assistere. Used in public services (social assistance), customer service, emergency response, and formal writing. More formal than "help" but broadly interchangeable in professional contexts. Phrases like "technical assistance" and "financial assistance" are standard in government and business. Neutral&nbsp;register.

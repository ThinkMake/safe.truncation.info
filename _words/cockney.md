definition: |
  Cockney refers to a person from the East End of London, or the dialect and accent associated with that community. Traditionally, a true Cockney is born within earshot of Bow Bells. Known for rhyming slang such as "plates of meat" for feet. From Middle English cokeney, a pampered child. The dialect carries strong working-class cultural&nbsp;identity.

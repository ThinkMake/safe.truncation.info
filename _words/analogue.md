definition: |
  Analogue is the British spelling of analog, referring to something representing variation through continuous physical properties rather than digits. Also used as a noun: one thing that parallels another in function or character. Common in electronics, chemistry, and comparison. American English typically uses the spelling&nbsp;analog.

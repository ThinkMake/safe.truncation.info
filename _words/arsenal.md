Arsenal originally meant a dockyard for building or storing ships and weapons, from Arabic dar as-sina'a, house of industry. Now refers broadly to a stock of weapons, or figuratively any collection of resources or skills. Used in military, sports, and metaphorical contexts. Arsenal is also a major English football&nbsp;club.

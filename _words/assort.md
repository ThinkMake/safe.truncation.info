definition: |
  Assort means to arrange or distribute into groups according to type or kind. From Old French assortir, to match. Used in manufacturing, retail, and classification contexts. Less common than its derivative "assorted," which describes a mixed collection. To assort items is to sort them by category. Formal and somewhat technical&nbsp;register.

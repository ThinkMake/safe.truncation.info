Dominion means sovereignty or control over a territory, or the territory itself. From Latin dominium, lordship. In British imperial history, a Dominion was a self-governing nation within the Commonwealth, such as Canada or Australia. Also used in theology and ecology. "Dominion over nature" is a biblical and philosophical concept. Formal&nbsp;register.

Cockle is a small edible saltwater bivalve mollusc with a ribbed, rounded shell, commonly eaten in Britain. From Old English coccel. Cockles are gathered from tidal flats and sold at seaside stalls. "Warm the cockles of one's heart" is a common idiom meaning to give deep comfort or pleasure. Plural:&nbsp;cockles.

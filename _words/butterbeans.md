definition: |
  Butterbeans are large, flat, creamy-white beans with a mild, buttery flavour. The term is used in the UK and southern US for different varieties: in the UK, butterbean typically refers to the lima bean; in the US South, it denotes a specific smaller variety. Used in stews, soups, and side dishes. High in protein and&nbsp;fibre.

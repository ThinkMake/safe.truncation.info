Subcompact describes a category of small cars smaller than compact cars, or any small version of a product class. From sub- plus compact. In automotive classification, subcompacts include models like the Ford Fiesta or Toyota Yaris. The term is also used in other industries for smaller-than-standard product sizes. American English usage; British English uses&nbsp;"supermini."

definition: |
  Subeditor is a journalist who checks and edits reporters' copy for publication, correcting errors, writing headlines, and preparing text for layout. From sub- plus editor. Standard in British newspaper and magazine publishing. The role is equivalent to "copy editor" in American English. Subeditors play a critical role in accuracy, style, and production. Abbreviated to&nbsp;"sub."

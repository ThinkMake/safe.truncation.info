Subzero describes temperatures below zero degrees, whether Celsius or Fahrenheit depending on context. From sub- plus zero. Used in weather reporting, food storage, and engineering. "Subzero temperatures" and "subzero conditions" are standard collocations. In North America especially common in weather contexts. Also a brand name applied to premium refrigerators.

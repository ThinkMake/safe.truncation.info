Analogical describes reasoning or expression based on analogy, drawing comparisons between similar structures or relationships. Used in philosophy, linguistics, and cognitive science to describe inference from parallel cases. Analogical reasoning underpins metaphor and model-building. Formal register; rarely used in casual&nbsp;conversation.

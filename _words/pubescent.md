definition: |
  Pubescent describes a person or organism that has reached or is undergoing puberty. From Latin pubescere, to reach maturity. Used in biology and medicine to indicate the onset of sexual maturity. In botany, pubescent describes a plant surface covered with fine, soft hair. Formal register; clinical and scientific in most contexts, occasionally used in literary&nbsp;description.

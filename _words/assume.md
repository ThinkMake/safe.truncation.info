definition: |
  Assume means to take something for granted without proof, or to take on a responsibility or role. From Latin assumere, to take up. Used across logic, business, law, and everyday speech. One assumes a fact, assumes command, or assumes a disguise. The noun form is assumption. Neutral register; context determines whether the meaning is cognitive or&nbsp;operational.

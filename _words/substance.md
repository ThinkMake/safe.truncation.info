definition: |
  Substance refers to a particular kind of matter with distinct properties, or the real meaning and importance of something. From Latin substantia, essence. Used in chemistry (a chemical substance), law (substance of an argument), and everyday speech. "Substance abuse" refers to harmful use of drugs or alcohol. "A person of substance" means someone significant. Spans multiple&nbsp;registers.

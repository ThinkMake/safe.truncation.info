definition: |
  Cockpit is the compartment where the pilot or driver sits in an aircraft, spacecraft, racing car, or boat. Originally referred to a pit used for cockfighting, later adopted by the Navy for the area where junior officers were stationed. Now standard aviation and motorsport terminology. Used figuratively for any centre of control or intense&nbsp;activity.

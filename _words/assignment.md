Assignment is a task or piece of work allocated to someone, or the legal transfer of a right or property. From Latin assignare. Common in education (homework assignment), journalism (a foreign assignment), and law (assignment of copyright). Also used in computing for variable assignment. Neutral register; widely used across professional and academic&nbsp;contexts.

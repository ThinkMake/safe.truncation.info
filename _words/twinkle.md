Twinkle means to shine with a gleam that changes repeatedly between bright and faint, or to move with a light rapid step. From Old English twinclian. Used of stars, lights, and eyes. "Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star" is one of the most recognised children's rhymes in English. As a noun, a twinkle is a gleam or sparkle. Warm, positive&nbsp;register.

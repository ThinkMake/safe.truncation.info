Asset is a useful or valuable thing, person, or quality. In finance, assets are items of economic value owned by an individual or organisation, listed on a balance sheet. From Anglo-Norman asetz, enough. Used in accounting, business, and general speech. "She's an asset to the team" is its common figurative use. Neutral&nbsp;register.

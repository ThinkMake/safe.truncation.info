definition: |
  Assault refers to a violent attack or the threat of one. As a legal term, it describes an act causing another person to apprehend imminent unlawful force. As a military term, it denotes a direct attack on a fortified position. From Old French assaut. Used in law, warfare, journalism, and everyday speech across all&nbsp;registers.

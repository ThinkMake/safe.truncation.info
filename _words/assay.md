definition: |
  Assay means to test or analyse the composition of a substance, especially a metal ore, to determine its purity or quality. From Old French essayer, to try. Used in metallurgy, chemistry, and pharmacology. As a noun, an assay is the test itself. Historically important in mining and coinage; now standard in laboratory&nbsp;science.

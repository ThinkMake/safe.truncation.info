  Cockatoo is a large crested parrot belonging to the family Cacatuidae, native to Australasia. From Malay kakatua. Notable for loud calls, strong beaks, and long lifespans of up to 80 years in some species. Popular but demanding pets. The sulphur-crested cockatoo is the most widely recognised species. All 21 species are native to Australia or nearby&nbsp;islands.

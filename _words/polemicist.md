definition: |
  Polemicist is a person who engages in or is skilled at writing polemics. From Greek polemikos. Polemicists argue aggressively and controversially, typically in print or public debate. The term can be neutral or mildly pejorative depending on context. Associated with religious controversy, political journalism, and intellectual combat. Related terms: polemical, polemic,&nbsp;polemics.

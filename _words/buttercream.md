definition: |
  Buttercream is a smooth, rich icing made from butter and icing sugar, used to decorate and fill cakes. Sometimes enriched with egg yolks, cream, or flavourings. Varieties include American (simple), Swiss meringue, Italian meringue, and French buttercream. A staple of professional and home baking. The name reflects its buttery texture and pale&nbsp;colour.

  Subjugate means to bring under domination or control, especially by conquest. From Latin subjugare, to put under a yoke. Used in history, politics, and figurative speech. Subjugation implies forceful suppression of freedom or autonomy. "Subjugated peoples," "subjugated desires." The noun is subjugation. Carries strong connotations of oppression and power.

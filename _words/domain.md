Domain means an area of land owned or controlled by a person or government, or a sphere of knowledge or activity. From Old French domaine, from Latin dominium, lordship. In computing, a domain is an internet address identifier. Used in law (public domain), science (domain of a function), and technology. Register ranges from formal legal to everyday figurative&nbsp;use.

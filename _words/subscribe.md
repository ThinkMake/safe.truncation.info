Subscribe means to arrange to receive a publication or service regularly, or to express agreement with a view. From Latin subscribere, to write beneath. Used in publishing, digital media, finance, and everyday speech. "Subscribe to a newsletter," "subscribe to a theory." Subscription is the noun form. Modern usage is dominated by streaming and software subscription&nbsp;models.

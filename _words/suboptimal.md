definition: |
  Suboptimal means less than the best possible; not fully optimal. From sub- plus optimal. Used in business, economics, computing, and everyday speech to describe solutions or outcomes that are adequate but not ideal. "A suboptimal decision" is a measured way of saying a poor choice was made. Neutral to mildly critical register; popular in professional and management&nbsp;contexts.

definition: |
  Subcellular describes structures, processes, or components existing within a cell, below the level of the whole cell. From sub- plus cellular. Used in cell biology and biochemistry to refer to organelles, membranes, and molecular machinery. Subcellular localisation identifies where proteins function within a cell. Strictly technical and scientific&nbsp;register.

definition: |
  Subatomic describes particles or phenomena smaller than an atom. From sub- (below) plus atomic. Subatomic particles include protons, neutrons, electrons, quarks, and bosons. The study of subatomic matter is the domain of particle physics. Used exclusively in scientific and technical contexts. The prefix sub- indicates a level below the named scale or&nbsp;category.

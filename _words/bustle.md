bad_word: "bust"
definition: |
  Bustle means to move energetically and noisily, or to cause someone to move hurriedly. As a noun, it refers to excited activity or, historically, a padded frame worn under a skirt to puff it out at the back, fashionable in the 1870s–80s. From a variant of obsolete busk, to prepare. Used in everyday speech and historical fashion&nbsp;contexts.

definition: |
  Assessment is the process or result of evaluating something systematically. From Latin assidere via assess. Used in education, medicine, risk management, taxation, and psychology. A risk assessment evaluates hazards; an educational assessment measures learning; a tax assessment determines liability. Plural: assessments. Neutral formal register across&nbsp;fields.
